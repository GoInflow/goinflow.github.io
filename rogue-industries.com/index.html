Welcome to Rogue Industries. We're a small, family-owned leather goods shop outside of Portland, Maine. We got our start with our innovative front pocket wallet designs, and now offer a full line of leather accessories from bags to briefcases. 

Remarkable leathers meet traditional craftsmanship.

Here at our workshop in Maine, we take the most remarkable leathers we can find to make goods that will last you for years. Our leather stitchers know what they're doing, and they impress us every day. Our Made in Maine Collection is driven by our appreciation of rugged, American style.
