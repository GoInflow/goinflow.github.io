About Penn State Industries

Family owned and operated since 1936, Penn State Industries has been assisting woodturners across the country for decades. When you want the best value, service and selection shop Pennstateind.com or call 1-800-377-7297 to place an order or to request a free catalog or free Pen Making DVD. Our on-line store offers great deals on high quality wood turning and pen making products including pen kits, pen turning supplies, pen blanks, mini lathes, lathe accessories, woodturning tools and lathe chucks. We hope you enjoy your on-line shopping experience at Penn State Industries.
